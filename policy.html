FAQs

How do I create an account?

Upon initial launch of the app, simply enter your name.

Do I need to make a password?

Currently, we do not store user information, but your own statistics are maintained locally on your device so there is no need for a password!

We are still working on a multiplayer feature and plan to release an update soon!
